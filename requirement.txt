Project requirement

**There will be two types of users:
    -Admin
    -Seller

**Seller can create "Offers"
**Offers will have following properties
    -Title,
    -Price,
    -Categories
    -Locations
    -Image(Optional)
    -Description
    -status
**Seller can edit,delete their Offers
**Seller can see the list of their Offers
**Admin can see the list of all the Offers
**Admin can edit, delete any Offers
